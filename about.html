About Us

Our Story

Founded on a passion for the sport and a commitment to community, Straight to the Point Archery has been helping archers find their focus since day one.

Mission & History

More Than a Sport

Straight to the Point Archery was founded with a simple belief: archery has something to offer everyone. Whether you're drawn to the meditative focus it requires, the thrill of competition, or simply the satisfaction of hitting a target, this sport builds character in ways few others can.

What began as a small community program has grown into a full-service archery center with structured classes, private instruction, and a welcoming community of archers at every level. We're proud to have introduced hundreds of students to the sport — and to have watched many of them go on to compete regionally and beyond.

Our mission is straightforward: provide world-class instruction in a safe, inclusive, and encouraging environment — and make the sport of archery accessible to everyone in our community.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Everything we do at Straight to the Point Archery is guided by three core values.

🎯

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standard in instruction, safety, and student experience. Our instructors are certified, experienced, and committed to continuous improvement.

🤝

Inclusion

Archery is for everyone. We actively welcome students of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities. Our environment is judgment-free and encouraging at every level.

🌱

Community

We're more than a training facility — we're a community. We foster lasting connections between our students and celebrate every milestone, from the first bullseye to the first tournament win.

Our Facilities

Built for Archers

Our facility is designed to support archers at every stage of their journey, with professional-grade equipment and a safe, well-maintained range.

🏹

Indoor Shooting Range

Our climate-controlled indoor range features multiple lanes with adjustable target distances from 10 to 30 yards, suitable for all class levels.

🗄️

Equipment Loaner Program

All equipment — bows, arrows, arm guards, and finger tabs — is provided for enrolled students. No purchase necessary to get started.

📋

Classroom Space

A dedicated classroom for instruction, video analysis, and equipment education. Theory and technique go hand in hand at our center.

🅿️

Free Parking

Ample free parking is available on-site for all students and visitors. We're conveniently located and easy to reach from across the community.
