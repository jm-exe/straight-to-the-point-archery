About Derek

Derek Jenkins is the founder and lead instructor of Straight to the Point Archery, bringing over 15 years of competitive archery experience to every class. A former collegiate competitor, Derek has coached students of all ages and skill levels — from first-time beginners to regional champions.

His coaching philosophy centers on patience, precision, and building genuine confidence. Derek believes that mastering the fundamentals is the key to long-term success, and he designs every class around deliberate, repeatable practice.

About Amy

Amy Smith discovered archery in high school and never looked back. After earning her USA Archery coaching certification, she joined Straight to the Point Archery with a focus on making the sport accessible and welcoming for newcomers — especially youth and adult beginners.

Amy's warm, encouraging teaching style creates a judgment-free environment where students feel comfortable making mistakes and learning from them. She specializes in working with beginners and is known for breaking down complex techniques into simple, actionable steps.

About Nathan

Nathan Lee is an avid competitive archer who has participated in state and regional tournaments for over a decade. His deep knowledge of equipment — particularly compound bows — makes him an invaluable resource for students looking to advance their technical understanding of the sport.

In the classroom, Nathan emphasizes the mental side of archery: focus, breathing, and composure under pressure. He works primarily with intermediate and advanced students who are looking to sharpen their consistency and start competing.
